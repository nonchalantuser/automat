Automat Industries was established in 1977 to cater to the engineering needs of various process industries. Headquartered at Plot No. 1032, G.I.D.C. Estate, Ankleshwar – 393002, Dist. Bharuch, Gujarat, India.

The company is led by Mr. Dilip Panchal, a Mechanical Engineering graduate with over 40 years of experience in the manufacturing of process plant equipment. Under his leadership, Automat Industries has grown into a trusted name across chemical, pharmaceutical, petrochemical, rubber, cement, bitumen, glass, and dairy industries.

"We go beyond manufacturing — we design, validate, deliver and stand firmly behind every solution."
