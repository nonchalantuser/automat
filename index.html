Automat Industries was established in 1977 to cater to the engineering needs of various process industries. Headquartered at Plot No. 1032, GIDC Industrial Area, Opp. Lalji Mulji Transport, Ankleshwar – 393002, Dist. Bharuch, Gujarat, India.

The company is led by Mr. Dilip Panchal, a Mechanical Engineering graduate with over 40 years of experience in the manufacturing of process plant equipment. Under his leadership, Automat Industries has grown into a trusted name across chemical, pharmaceutical, rubber, cement, bitumen, glass, and dairy industries.

We manufacture both ferrous and non-ferrous equipment using materials such as SS316, SS304, Carbon Steel, Copper, Inconel, and Hastelloy — all designed to meet ASME, TEMA, API, DIN and Indian Standards (IS) codes. Our quality is verified by agencies including Bureau Veritas, TUV, and Tata Consulting Engineers.

What We Make

Our Product Range

Comprehensive industrial solutions engineered with precision, built to international standards.

Reactors (Limpeted & Jacketed)

Limpeted and jacketed reactors for chemical processes. Capacities up to 40 KL, with sizes ranging from 100 mm to 3500 mm ID, in SS316, SS304, and Carbon Steel.

  • Capacity up to 40 KL
  • Size: 100 mm – 3500 mm ID
  • ASME certified design

Storage Tanks

Large-capacity storage tanks for chemicals and process fluids. Capacities up to 120 KL with max. diameter up to 5500 mm, in SS304, SS316, and Carbon Steel.

  • Capacity up to 120 KL
  • Max. diameter: 5500 mm
  • IS / ASME compliant

Distillation Columns

Precision-engineered distillation columns for separation processes, suitable for chemical and petrochemical applications requiring high purity outputs.

  • Packed & Tray types
  • High-purity separation
  • Custom internals available

Autoclaves

High-pressure autoclaves for sterilization and chemical synthesis, built to withstand extreme pressures and temperatures with precision safety controls.

  • High-pressure rated
  • Safety valve integrated
  • ASME / DIN compliant

Pressure Vessels

Custom pressure vessels for storing gases and liquids at high pressure, manufactured per ASME Section VIII standards for maximum safety and reliability.

  • ASME Sec. VIII certified
  • NDT tested
  • All pressure ratings

Condensers & Coolers

Industrial condensers and coolers designed for efficient heat transfer, suitable for chemical vapor condensation and industrial cooling applications.

  • Shell & tube design
  • High thermal efficiency
  • TEMA standards

Screw Conveyors

Helical screw conveyors for bulk material transport. Max. length up to 20 metres, max. diameter up to 600 mm × 16 mm thickness. U-type, flared, rectangular & tubular troughs.

  • Max. length: 20 metres
  • Max. dia: 600 mm × 16 mm
  • Horizontal & inclined designs

Belt Conveyors

Heavy-duty belt conveyor systems for transporting bulk materials across long distances. Used extensively in cement, mining, and aggregate industries.

  • High load capacity
  • Long-span configurations
  • Variable speed drives

Roller Conveyors

Gravity and powered roller conveyors for efficient material handling in manufacturing and packaging lines. Available in various roller diameters and spacing configurations.

  • Gravity & motorized types
  • Custom roller spacing
  • Low maintenance design

Bucket Elevators

Vertical bucket elevators for lifting bulk materials. Max. height up to 30 metres. Single and double chain types with fabricated, cast iron, and hardened bucket options.

  • Max. height: 30 metres
  • Single & double chain types
  • Fabricated, CI & hardened buckets
